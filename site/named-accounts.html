      ▸ Denominator & disclosure

      White space is measured against the 450 assessed doors — dispensaries with enough priced shelf data to classify (≥5 priced items, not zero-row).       ▸ Reading the matrix

      The Curaleaf column (coral) is the insight axis — absence of a coral mark is the opportunity. A blank in a competitor column means that competitor is absent from that door, not missing data. Tracked-competitor coverage is uneven by state: Trulieve shows zero across the pilot panel and Ascend is effectively absent from New York, so their columns read empty in NY by reality, not omission.
