Prepared for

Which retailers are off-band this week?

Until Curaleaf provides MSRP, we model Synthetic MSRP from two anchors: (1) median price at Curaleaf-owned stores (ground truth) when N≥3 stores carry the matched SKU, otherwise (2) cross-retailer modal price. Products are matched on brand × product type × pack size × THC band. Retailers are flagged at four severity bands (±5/10/15/25%) against the Synthetic MSRP.

▸ Three brand-wide signals this week

Find NY: Curaleaf-owned anchor $19.50 for 3.5g flower. 10 of 12 NY retailers pricing 25%+ above — market median $30, a 54% premium over Curaleaf-retail. Same pattern Select 1g vape NY ($35 anchor vs $59.65 market, 7 of 8 extreme).
Find NJ: Curaleaf-owned anchor $12.00 for 1g pre-roll. 5 of 6 NJ retailers pricing 15-25% BELOW — opposite-direction excursion from NY. Brand-wide pricing discipline issue.
Grassroots IL: Tightest discipline in the pilot. 11 of 14 retailers in-band for 3.5g flower at $32.50. Reference case for what good looks like.

Methodology · Synthetic MSRP construction

How we model implied MSRP without an MSRP feed
RuleAnchorMinimumConfidence
1. Curaleaf-owned modalMedian price at Curaleaf-owned stores carrying the matched SKUN ≥ 3 Curaleaf-owned storesHIGH (Curaleaf-owned)
2. Cross-retailer modalMedian across all non-Curaleaf retailers carrying matched SKUN ≥ 10 obs & ≥ 3 distinct retailersHIGH (market modal)
3. Trimmed-mean fallback10% trimmed mean (trim 10% from each tail)N ≥ 10 obs (no modal ≥ 30%)MEDIUM
4. Multi-state escalationPilot-wide modal across IL/NJ/NYState N < 10 obsMEDIUM (thin market)
5. Persistence ruleSynthetic MSRP does not change unless new modal persists 8 weeks—Anti-drift

Matching rules. brand_canonical (Curaleaf graph: Select, Grassroots, Find, Anthem, JAMS, Reef, B Noble); product_type (vape / flower / pre-roll / edible / concentrate); pack_size (weight_g or size_mg, parsed at extraction); THC band (quintile bins per category, e.g. flower 18-22% / 22-26% / 26-30% / 30%+). Unmatched cohorts are excluded from MSRP modeling.

Severity bands. ±5% in-band (not flagged). ±5-10% soft. ±10-15% hard (fires P2 alert). ±15-25% severe (fires P1 alert). >±25% extreme (fires P0 alert). Both above-band and below-band excursions are reported.

▸ Once Curaleaf provides MSRP

The Synthetic MSRP becomes the fallback, not the primary anchor. Real MSRP plugs into the same severity-band framework on the same page. The Curaleaf-owned modal becomes a secondary read — useful for detecting whether Curaleaf retail itself is on-MSRP. The architecture does not change; only the anchor source does.
