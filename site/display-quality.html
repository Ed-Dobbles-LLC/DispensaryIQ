▸ How to read these metrics

Shelf score is computed from product position within its category section — 100 means the brand's SKUs sit at the top of every section, 1 means buried at the bottom. Promo intensity is observed at capture — high values mean the brand leans on discount mechanics; low values may mean premium positioning OR weak promotional activation. Featured share tracks editorial picks (Staff Pick, Featured, Manager's Choice) — signal of earned merchandising. The interesting Curaleaf insight: shelf score sits at 48, meaning portfolio products land mid-page rather than top-of-section — an addressable shelf-execution opportunity for the trade-marketing team.
