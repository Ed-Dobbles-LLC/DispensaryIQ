▸ Methodology

Anchor. Median price across Curaleaf-owned stores carrying this SKU in the selected state. Verdict compares the Curaleaf-owned median-effective to the cross-retailer market-effective median (independents + non-Curaleaf chains).
Regular vs Effective. Regular = median list price (MSRP) before promos. Effective = median price customers actually pay after observed discounts. The gap between the two is the promo intensity in that cell.
Promo participation. Share of priced observations in the cell that were on sale (price < regular). Average discount depth = mean of discount_pct across those on-promo observations.
Bands. ±5% in-line. 5–15% slight premium / value. >15% material premium / value. Below-market is reported as a positive (Curaleaf retail underpricing the market is a competitive position, not a problem).
Coverage. Quality filter: dispensaries with ≥5 priced items on their menu, aggregator websites excluded. Cells without a Curaleaf-owned store fall back to market spread for context. Empty cells indicate no qualifying observations.
