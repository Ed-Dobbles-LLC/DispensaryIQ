▸ Methodology

Curaleaf median. Median price across Curaleaf-owned pricing observations for this brand · category cohort in the selected state, as reported by the live pricing feed.
Market median. Median price across qualifying non-Curaleaf market observations for the same cohort and state.
Delta vs market & positioning. Computed upstream by the certified pricing pipeline and passed through unchanged — this page does not re-derive them from the raw medians.
Coverage. Cohorts without a Curaleaf-owned observation fall back to market spread for context. Empty cells indicate no qualifying observations met the feed’s minimum-observation threshold this week.
