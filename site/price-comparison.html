▸ Bottom-line up-front

Across the seven pilot cells with a Curaleaf-owned anchor, Curaleaf is priced at or below the market median in five, slightly above in one (Grassroots 3.5g flower IL, +13%), and meaningfully above in one (Find 3.5g flower NJ, +25%). The story for the pitch: Curaleaf retail is generally a value play, not the source of off-band pricing — that pressure sits with the independents.

▸ Methodology

Anchor. Mean price across Curaleaf-owned stores carrying the matched SKU in the selected state. Verdict pill compares the Curaleaf-owned mean to the cross-retailer market median (independents + non-Curaleaf chains).
Bands. ±5% in-line. 5–15% slight premium / value. >15% material premium / value. Below-market is reported as a positive (Curaleaf retail underpricing the market is a competitive position, not a problem).
Coverage. Cells without a Curaleaf-owned store (Select 2g vape NJ & NY, Anthem 1g pre-roll all states, Grassroots NJ/NY, JAMS NJ) show market-only context until Curaleaf provides MSRP. SKUs matched on brand × product type × pack size; pricing data from license-reconciled IL/NJ/NY pilot universe.
