▸ Why mean and median diverge

119 dispensaries (12%) carry 1,000+ products — these are platform-API hubs (Dutchie, iHeartJane, Verano gateway) where full catalog extraction lands the entire SKU range. 519 dispensaries (52%) carry under 50 products — these are LLM-extracted captures where typical menu depth surfaces. For pitch-quality reporting, use the median (46) as the typical dispensary; use the mean (391) only when describing total intelligence depth.
